# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") establishes guidelines for permissible and prohibited uses of Lead Scraper. Violations may result in account suspension or termination. This Policy supplements our Terms of Service and applies to all users.

---

## 2. Prohibited Uses

### 2.1 Illegal Activities

You are prohibited from using Lead Scraper for any activity that violates local, state, federal, or international law, including:

**Data Collection Violations**
- Scraping websites in violation of their Terms of Service or robots.txt
- Accessing password-protected pages or non-public data
- Violating Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
- Bypassing security measures or authentication systems
- Scraping government-restricted databases
- Collecting data protected by non-disclosure agreements

**Privacy & Data Protection Violations**
- Collecting personal data without legal basis
- Violating GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, PIPEDA, or other privacy laws
- Violating CAN-SPAM Act, GDPR marketing rules, CASL
- Violating Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA)
- Accessing confidential business information unlawfully
- Trading in stolen or fraudulently obtained data

**Fraud & Impersonation**
- Impersonating individuals or organizations
- Creating fake accounts or misrepresenting identity
- Committing wire fraud, mail fraud, or identity theft
- Phishing or social engineering attacks
- Violating fair lending or credit discrimination laws (FCRA, FDCPA, Fair Housing Act)

**Other Legal Violations**
- Violating wiretapping or surveillance laws
- Engaging in money laundering or sanctions evasion
- Exporting controlled technology unlawfully
- Harassing, threatening, or abusing individuals

### 2.2 Anti-Spam & Anti-Abuse

**No Spam**
You agree NOT to:
- Send unsolicited bulk email to leads
- Violate CAN-SPAM Act (requires valid sender, opt-out, subject line honesty)
- Violate GDPR direct marketing rules (requires consent)
- Violate CASL (Canada's anti-spam law)
- Send more than 5 messages without explicit consent per contact
- Use Lead Scraper to mass-mail without consent infrastructure
- Ignore unsubscribe or opt-out requests

**No Harassment or Threats**
You agree NOT to:
- Send threatening, abusive, or hateful messages
- Engage in cyberstalking or harassment
- Target individuals based on protected characteristics
- Make credible threats of violence
- Send repeated unwanted contact (more than 2 times after clear refusal)
- Impersonate law enforcement or government officials

**No Abuse of Service**
You agree NOT to:
- Use automated tools to probe, scan, or test Service vulnerabilities
- Engage in denial-of-service (DoS) or distributed attacks
- Perform activities that degrade Service for other users
- Bypass rate limiting or throttling mechanisms
- Create multiple accounts to evade restrictions
- Interfere with Service infrastructure or security

### 2.3 Competitive Intelligence & Misuse

**Prohibited Commercial Uses**
You agree NOT to:
- Scrape competitors' leads and contact lists for intelligence
- Create lists specifically to harm competitors' businesses
- Violate trade secret or confidentiality laws
- Reverse-engineer or replicate competitors' products
- Use leads to interfere with business relationships (tortious interference)
- Target leads identified by competitors for harassment purposes

**Unethical Uses**
You agree NOT to:
- Use leads for market manipulation or pump-and-dump schemes
- Conduct predatory lending using scraped leads
- Target vulnerable populations (elderly, disabled, low-income) for schemes
- Use leads for discrimination in hiring, lending, or housing
- Conduct price-gouging or predatory practices with leads
- Use for illegal gambling or betting operations

### 2.4 Intellectual Property Violations

You agree NOT to:
- Scrape copyrighted content from websites
- Violate patent rights of third parties
- Use trademarked terms without authorization
- Infringe on public figures' rights of publicity
- Scrape and resell original databases
- Redistribute proprietary lead lists

### 2.5 Data Mishandling

**Inadequate Security**
- Storing scraped data insecurely
- Failing to encrypt sensitive lead information
- Sharing data with unauthorized parties
- Storing data in countries with inadequate protection
- Leaving credentials exposed (API keys in public repos)

**Improper Data Practices**
- Combining Lead Scraper data with other databases to create surveillance profiles
- Using data for purposes not disclosed to data subjects
- Failing to honor data deletion requests
- Retaining data beyond stated retention periods
- Using data for automated decision-making without oversight

## 3. Restricted Uses (Require Special Approval)

The following uses are restricted and require explicit written approval:

### 3.1 High-Risk Industries
- **Political campaigns** - Must comply with election laws
- **Financial services** - Must comply with securities and lending laws
- **Healthcare** - Must comply with HIPAA
- **Insurance** - Must comply with fair dealing laws
- **Payday lending** - Must avoid predatory targeting

### 3.2 Data Sensitivity
- **Combining with sensitive data** - Medical history, mental health, criminal records
- **Children's data** - Any use involving minors requires COPPA compliance
- **Protected class data** - Race, religion, disability, sexual orientation-based targeting
- **Financial vulnerability** - Targeting based on credit scores, bankruptcy, foreclosure

### 3.3 Geographic Restrictions
- **GDPR data** - EU/EEA processing must meet specific safeguards
- **California residents** - CCPA restrictions apply
- **China/Russia/Iran** - US sanctions compliance required
- **Sanctioned countries** - OFAC/export control compliance

### 3.4 How to Request Approval
Email: compliance@leadscraper.io

Include:
- Intended use case in detail
- Data types and geographies
- Legal basis and compliance plan
- Company background and industry
- Data security measures

Approval typically takes 5-7 business days.

## 4. Enforcement & Violations

### 4.1 Monitoring

Lead Scraper actively monitors for violations through:
- **Automated scanning** - Flagging suspicious patterns (bulk contacts, mass emails)
- **Rate limiting** - Detecting and blocking rate limit breaches
- **Abuse reports** - User-submitted complaints and regulatory referrals
- **Pattern detection** - Machine learning for phishing, spam, harassment
- **Periodic reviews** - Manual audits of high-volume accounts

### 4.2 Warning System

**First Violation (Minor)**
- Automated warning email
- Review of acceptable use with user
- No service disruption
- 30-day observation period

**Second Violation or Serious First Violation**
- Formal notice from compliance team
- Requirement to remediate within 10 days
- Rate limiting or feature restriction applied
- 60-day observation period
- Possible suspension if not corrected

**Severe or Repeated Violations**
- Immediate account suspension (24-48 hours)
- Data access revoked
- Content deleted or archived
- Legal action may be initiated
- No refund of prepaid fees
- Law enforcement notification (if criminal)

### 4.3 Immediate Suspension (No Warning)

The following warrant immediate suspension:
- Child exploitation, trafficking, or grooming
- Credible threats of violence
- Targeted harassment campaigns
- Active fraud or financial crimes
- DDOS or security attacks
- Repeat major violations within 12 months

### 4.4 Termination

We will terminate accounts for:
- Violations of laws (fraud, illegal scraping, etc.)
- Repeated abuse after warnings
- Severe harassment or threats
- Non-payment of invoices (30 days past due)
- Loss of confidence in user's compliance
- Court order or regulatory direction

Termination results in:
- Immediate account deletion
- Data purged (no recovery option)
- IP address and payment method blocklisted
- Possible legal action if criminal violations
- No refund of prepaid fees

### 4.5 Appeal Process

If your account is suspended, you may appeal within 10 days:

**Appeal Submission:**
Email: appeals@leadscraper.io

Include:
- Your account email
- Description of suspension
- Your explanation of the situation
- Evidence of compliance (if applicable)
- Remediation steps you've taken

**Appeal Review:**
- Manual review by compliance team (5-7 business days)
- Decision made independently of original determination
- If upheld, you may not reapply for 12 months
- If reversed, account restored with clean slate

**Note:** Appeals don't override legal/regulatory obligations. We must comply with law enforcement and regulatory orders regardless of user appeal.

## 5. Specific Use Case Guidelines

### 5.1 B2B Lead Generation (✓ Permitted)

**What's OK:**
- Scraping business contact info from public directories
- Using leads for direct sales outreach (with consent infrastructure)
- Combining with public company data
- AI-scoring leads for qualification

**What's NOT OK:**
- Scraping non-public data (password-protected areas)
- Ignoring unsubscribe requests
- Misrepresenting company or offer
- Mass emailing without consent mechanism

### 5.2 Market Research (✓ Permitted)

**What's OK:**
- Analyzing aggregate trends (e.g., "insurance companies hiring")
- Academic research (published with proper attribution)
- Industry benchmarking (anonymized comparisons)

**What's NOT OK:**
- Identifying and contacting individuals without disclosure
- Using for competitive targeting
- Republishing original data without attribution

### 5.3 Recruiting & HR (✓ Permitted)

**What's OK:**
- Sourcing candidate information from public profiles
- Enriching candidate records with public data
- Outreach for genuine job opportunities

**What's NOT OK:**
- Scraping LinkedIn profiles in violation of their ToS
- Targeting protected characteristics (age discrimination)
- Violating Fair Housing Act in hiring
- Misrepresenting job opportunities

### 5.4 Real Estate (⚠️ Restricted - Approval Required)

**Requirements:**
- Fair Housing Act compliance (no discrimination)
- TCPA compliance for phone contacts
- Truth in Lending Act compliance
- State real estate law compliance

**What's NOT OK:**
- Targeting by race, ethnicity, religion, disability
- Predatory lending practices
- Privacy violations in property searches

### 5.5 Insurance (⚠️ Restricted - Approval Required)

**Requirements:**
- Fair dealing law compliance
- No discrimination based on protected characteristics
- FCRA/credit score compliance
- State insurance law compliance

**What's NOT OK:**
- Using data to deny coverage unfairly
- Discriminatory underwriting practices

### 5.6 Financial Services (⚠️ Restricted - Approval Required)

**Requirements:**
- FCRA compliance
- Fair Lending Act compliance
- FDCPA (Fair Debt Collection) compliance
- Securities law compliance

**What's NOT OK:**
- Predatory lending
- Discriminatory lending
- Unsolicited investment offers
- Pump-and-dump schemes

### 5.7 Prohibited: Adult/Sexual Content

**Never Permitted:**
- Using data for adult services or sex work
- Creating profiles for sexual exploitation
- Scraping for prostitution, trafficking, or solicitation
- Non-consensual intimate imagery

### 5.8 Prohibited: Political Campaigns & Voter Suppression

**Prohibited:**
- Using leads for voter suppression
- Targeting voters to discourage participation
- Spreading disinformation with scraped leads
- Violating campaign finance laws
- Unauthorized political campaigning

## 6. Data Subject Rights

### 6.1 Our Responsibility
As the data processor, Lead Scraper ensures:
- Your data is processed lawfully
- Data subjects can exercise rights (access, deletion, portability)
- Privacy is respected and laws are followed

### 6.2 Your Responsibility
As the data controller, you must:
- Obtain consents before contacting (where required)
- Respond to data subject rights requests within 10 days
- Provide privacy notices before collecting data
- Respect unsubscribe and opt-out requests
- Delete data when requested

### 6.3 Consequences of Non-Compliance
If you fail to respect data subject rights:
- We will disable your Service access
- We'll honor the data subject's deletion request directly
- You remain liable to regulators and data subjects
- We may report to authorities if criminal
