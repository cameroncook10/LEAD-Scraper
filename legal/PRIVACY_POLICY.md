## 8. Your Data Rights

### 8.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Residents)

You have the right to:

**Access (Article 15)**
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Understand what data we hold and how we use it
- Response time: 30 days

**Rectification (Article 16)**
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- We will update records and notify relevant parties

**Erasure (Article 17)**
- Request deletion of your data
- Subject to legal retention requirements
- "Right to be forgotten" applies in most cases

**Restriction (Article 18)**
- Limit processing of your data
- We'll store but not actively process it

**Portability (Article 20)**
- Receive your data in a portable format
- Transfer to another service provider

**Objection (Article 21)**
- Opt-out of marketing communications (always free)
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Withdraw consent anytime

**Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)**
- Object to decisions based solely on automated processing
- Request human review of automated decisions

### 8.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

You have the right to:

**Know (§1798.100)**
- What personal information we collect
- How we use it
- Who we share it with

**Delete (§1798.105)**
- Request deletion of collected personal information
- Exception: Legal retention obligations

**Opt-Out (§1798.120)**
- Opt-out of "selling" or "sharing" personal information
- We provide a "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link

**Non-Discrimination (§1798.125)**
- We won't discriminate against you for exercising rights
- No denial of service or price changes for privacy choices

**Limit Use (§1798.121)**
- Limit use to service delivery purposes
- Opt-out of behavioral advertising

### 8.3 LGPD Rights (Brazil)

You can:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion
- Opt-out of non-essential processing
- Withdraw consent
- Request processing limitations

### 8.4 How to Exercise Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact:

**Email:** privacy@leadscraper.io
**Subject:** "[REQUEST TYPE] - Data Subject Request"

Requests must include:
- Your name and account email
- Specific right you're exercising
- Proof of identity (for verification)

We will respond within:
- **GDPR:** 30 days (extendable to 90 days)
- **CCPA:** 45 days (extendable to 60 days)
- **LGPD:** 15 days (extendable to 15 additional days)

## 9. Data Security

### 9.1 Security Measures

We implement:
- **Encryption:** AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection
- **Audits:** Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
- **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
- **Employee Training:** Regular security awareness training

### 9.2 API Key Protection
- Keys are hashed before storage
- Never shared in logs or emails
- Rotatable via dashboard
- Automatic rotation recommended every 90 days

### 9.3 Breach Response
In case of a security breach:
1. We'll investigate immediately
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR) or 60 days (CCPA)
3. Provide guidance on protective measures
4. Notify regulators as required by law
5. Publish transparency reports

### 9.4 What We Cannot Guarantee
- We cannot guarantee 100% security
- No internet-connected service is completely secure
- Use strong passwords and enable 2FA
